## How Does Universal ERP Link Work?

Connecting TRES to your chosen ERP is extremely simple:

- Users have to select our Universal ERP as their chosen integration option.

- As part of the initial set-up, customers have to upload your Chart Of Accounts (COA) to TRES. While not necessary, this is a good moment to create auto-labeled rules to automate the transaction categorization to your chart of accounts, saving time and simplifying your day to day.

- Once your account is set, users will be able to leverage the full power of TRES FinOS, using blockchain-verified data, automating accounting and consuming structured reports for audit, financial performance and reconciliation purposes.

- To synchronize your journal entries with ERP, users just need to create a Pre-Sync Report and upload it to their chosen ERP. This report, which can be exported in .csv or .xls format.
