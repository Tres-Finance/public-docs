# Setting a New Standard for Exchange Transparency: TRES x BitMEX

As the digital asset market matures, the expectations around transparency and financial accuracy are evolving just as quickly.

BitMEX has integrated with the TRES Financial Data Lake, connecting directly to enterprise-grade, audit-ready financial data built for digital asset operations at scale.

### Raising the Standard for Exchange Transparency

As regulatory frameworks mature, the gap between technical performance and financial precision is closing fast. Exchanges are expected to demonstrate not just liquidity and speed, but also the ability to produce verifiable, compliant financial records.

That requires infrastructure designed for complexity – where blockchain, custodial, and fiat data intersect seamlessly.

TRES provides exactly that: an intelligent data layer that normalizes, reconciles, and structures every transaction into a single source of financial truth.

### The TRES Financial Data Lake

At the core of TRES is a platform purpose-built for the realities of digital asset finance – where multi-chain activity, custodians, and DeFi interact with traditional systems. The TRES Financial Data Lake aggregates, normalizes, and reconciles this data across hundreds of sources, giving exchanges and institutions a complete, compliant financial picture.

By integrating with TRES, BitMEX joins a growing network of exchanges, custodians, and service providers that are TRES-certified and working toward the same goal: trusted, verifiable financial data for the digital asset economy.

### Looking Ahead

TRES powers financial data infrastructure for exchanges, custodians, and financial institutions globally. With BitMEX now connected, another major exchange joins the network of platforms using TRES to bring more clarity, consistency, and confidence to digital asset finance.

Together, TRES and BitMEX are setting a higher bar for how exchanges operate, report, and build trust in the next era of finance.
