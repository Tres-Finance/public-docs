# Revolutionizing Web3 Financial Reporting: TRES Finance Partners with Fireblocks

We’re proud to announce a groundbreaking new partnership between TRES Finance and Fireblocks, the leading provider of digital asset security and operational infrastructure for institutions.

At TRES Finance, we know that getting contextual financial data for your full Web3 activity is absolutely critical to fulfilling your accounting, audit and reporting requirements. We’ve partnered with Fireblocks to bring the power of our Financial Data Lake to users across Fireblocks’ suite of tools and services.

Starting today, as a Fireblocks customer, you’ll be able to request a TRES report directly from the Fireblocks platform. This collaboration is the first of its kind for Fireblocks, and we’re proud to be supporting their huge institutional user base with TRES’ powerful, contextual Web3 financial reporting.

“We’re super excited to partner with Fireblocks, as the leader in their space we’re now enabling their customers to take advantage of our powerful Web3 financial reporting, accounting and audit workflows. As a company, TRES shares a similar mission to Fireblocks, and we’re looking forward to collaborating even further in the future.”

“Our customers have many reporting and accounting requirements, with activity across all of Fireblocks’ supported assets. This made it a no-brainer for us to partner with TRES. Not only did they cover every asset for our customer base, but they also have a super passionate and responsive team.”

At TRES, we simplify Web3 financial operations and make accounting, audit and reporting the easiest part of your financial workflow. By joining forces with Fireblocks, we’re able to collaborate on their mission to simplify working with digital assets.

Fireblocks has established itself as a leader in digital asset security, providing institutional-grade solutions that protect cryptocurrencies and digital assets against the ever-present threat of cyberattacks and unauthorized access. Their robust infrastructure and multi-layered security protocols have set the standard for the industry, earning the trust of some of the most discerning players in the digital asset ecosystem.

Together, we’re committed to bridging the gap between traditional finance and the decentralized future, unlocking new opportunities and capabilities for our institutional users. Through this collaboration, we aim to catalyze the widespread adoption of digital assets and drive positive change in the global financial landscape.
