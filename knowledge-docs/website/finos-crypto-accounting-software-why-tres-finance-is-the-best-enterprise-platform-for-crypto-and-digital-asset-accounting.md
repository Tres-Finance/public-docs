## Key Features of the Platform

### A Financial Data Lake for Digital Assets

TRES Finance connects to 200+ blockchains, exchanges, custodians, and banks. All data flows into a single Financial Data Lake. The platform enriches, reconciles, and structures the data automatically. Output is accurate, audit-ready financial information, delivered in real time.

### Multi-Source Reconciliation

Reconciling across wallets, exchanges, custodians, and banks is complex. TRES Finance uses an AI engine to automate this process. It matches transactions across five dimensions: token amounts, fiat values, transaction counts, cost basis, and ending balances. Accuracy exceeds 99%. Unmatched items are flagged for review, with full traceability. Every transaction is accounted for and audit-ready.

### Automation Across Every Workflow

TRES Finance automates DeFi activity, staking rewards, cross-chain transfers, and fund structures. Transactions are tagged, categorized, and converted into compliant journal entries. The system supports FIFO, LIFO, GAAP, and IFRS, covering global reporting needs.

### ERP Integration Without Limits

Most platforms connect to only a few ERP systems. TRES Finance connects to any ERP worldwide. This includes NetSuite, QuickBooks, Xero, and regional or industry-specific systems. Digital assets can be managed inside the same workflows as traditional finance. No brittle connectors. No custom builds.

### Compliance You Can Rely On

TRES Finance holds SOC 1 and SOC 2 Type 2 certifications. Proof of Funds enables instant reconciliation across millions of wallets. Auditors and regulators gain direct, verifiable transparency.

### AI-Powered Finance Insights

TRES Analyst allows natural-language queries. Example: “What were our staking rewards last quarter?” The system returns immediate, accurate answers with visualizations. Automated risk detection flags spam tokens and suspicious airdrops before they impact reporting.

### Invoicing and Billing for Hybrid Finance

TRES Finance includes native invoicing and billing for fiat and crypto. Payments are automatically reconciled, tagged to the chart of accounts, and logged for audits. Manual work in hybrid finance is eliminated.

### The Bottom Line

TRES Finance is built for enterprises, not hobbyists. It delivers accuracy, compliance, and automation at scale. With broad coverage, universal ERP integration, advanced reconciliation, and certified compliance, it sets the standard for digital asset accounting.
