# TRES Launches MCP AI Aggregator with Fireblocks Integration

We’re excited to announce the launch of the TRES MCP AI Aggregator – a secure API interface that allows AI systems to connect directly to financial tools, systems, and data sources through a single, unified endpoint.

Built on the emerging Model Context Protocol (MCP), it provides a safe, consistent framework for connecting AI agents to finance – and we’re proud to launch with deep Fireblocks integration included from day one. That means AI agents can now tap into secure, real-time wallet and transaction data from Fireblocks, the gold standard in digital asset security, alongside the broader financial data already flowing through TRES.

### Why This Matters

At TRES, we sit with CFOs and finance leaders every day. What we hear is consistent: the role has never been more demanding.

Finance teams in digital assets are juggling hundreds of wallets, staking across protocols, preparing for audits on unfamiliar assets, and keeping everything compliant across multiple jurisdictions.

Now, boards are asking about AI. CFOs want the efficiency, but they can’t afford experiments.

Finance doesn’t get do-overs. Compliance, audit readiness, and transparency can’t be compromised just to test new tech.

That’s why we built the MCP AI Aggregator: a way to adopt AI responsibly, with compliance at the core.

### What It Unlocks for Finance Teams

Instead of bolting AI onto finance workflows and hoping for the best, CFOs now have a framework to use AI with confidence.

Imagine:

- A CFO asks: What were our staking rewards last quarter, broken down by chain? Instead of juggling reports, AI delivers a structured, audit-ready answer instantly.

- Treasury teams route AI prompts directly to live wallet balances and reconciliations, enabling real-time liquidity management.

- Compliance leads automate monitoring, exception handling, and reporting – with full traceability. Every action logged. Every answer auditable.

It doesn’t stop at single use cases. The aggregator can orchestrate multiple systems at once – pulling wallet data from Fireblocks, reconciling it in TRES, and generating a draft audit report in minutes.

### The Bigger Picture

We’re not giving finance teams another shiny tool. We’re giving them a way to use AI responsibly – informed by the right data, wrapped in the right controls, and connected to systems they already trust.

This is the future of financial operations: AI that isn’t just interesting in a demo, but usable in production – all backed by our enterprise-grade platform.
