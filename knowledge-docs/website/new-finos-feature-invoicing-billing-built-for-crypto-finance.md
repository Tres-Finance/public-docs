## A New Era of Invoicing and Billing for Crypto Finance

Today we’re introducing native invoicing and billing inside TRES, a unified solution for creating, tracking, and reconciling both invoices and bills across fiat and crypto.

With this launch, finance teams can now:

- Generate and send branded invoices with both fiat and crypto payment options.

- Manage vendor bills in the same platform, tagged to chart of accounts.

- Preserve ERP-compliant identifiers and jurisdictional controls.

- Maintain audit-ready logs across every receivable, payable, and status update.

## How Does TRES Invoicing Work?

You can now create an invoice or bill directly from TRES.

For receivables, simply add line items, embed wallet and bank instructions, and issue a branded invoice. TRES handles sequencing and compliance.

For payables, upload or enter a bill, tag the appropriate account and payment method, and push to your ERP.

TRES also lets you manage providers and customers directly in the platform, keeping workflows consistent across teams. A centralized dashboard shows all invoices and bills in one place, making audits and period-end reviews faster and more reliable.
