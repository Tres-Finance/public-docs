# Secure DeFi, Simplified: TRES Finance Teams Up with Fordefi

We’re excited to announce our new partnership with Fordefi, a leader in institutional-grade MPC wallet solutions that offer advanced security controls, granular permissions and robust transaction policy oversight.

As institutions expand their presence in Web3, the need for secure asset management and transparent financial reporting has never been greater. Fordefi protects your digital assets while TRES Finance aggregates on-chain data into structured, audit-ready insights that simplify compliance and financial operations.

This integration is designed with institutions in mind. By granting TRES Finance read-only access to your Fordefi environment, you continue to maintain strict control over asset movements while gaining real-time financial visibility and enhanced reporting capabilities. The result is a comprehensive solution that minimizes operational risk and empowers you to navigate the complexities of digital assets with confidence.
