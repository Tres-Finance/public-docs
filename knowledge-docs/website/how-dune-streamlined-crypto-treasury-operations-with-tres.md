# How Dune Streamlined Crypto Treasury Operations with TRES

## The Challenge: Bringing Clarity and Control to On-Chain Treasury Activity

As one of the most prominent players in blockchain analytics, Dune operates deeply within the crypto ecosystem – not just analyzing networks, but also actively managing on-chain assets as part of its treasury and operations.

With payments, consulting expenses, and treasury diversification happening across multiple wallets and blockchains, Dune needed a robust solution to streamline reconciliation, ensure data integrity, and build audit-ready reports.

“We knew we needed a system that could keep pace with the ecosystem we operate in – TRES gave us that visibility and control.” – Aleksander Langmyhr, Director of Finance at Dune.

### The Solution: A Unified View of Treasury with TRES

Dune adopted TRES to simplify its month-end financial processes and ensure accurate reporting of digital asset activity. The platform now plays a central role in helping the finance team and their accounting partners stay aligned across every wallet and transaction.

With TRES, Dune is able to:

- Centralize visibility across wallets and supported blockchains.

- Reconcile crypto transactions from customer payments to vendor activity.

- Export standardized financial data into Xledger, their ERP of choice.

- Track staking positions and rewards across various protocols.

“It’s a significant improvement from digging through block explorers. TRES lets us track and validate transactions with confidence.”

### The Impact: Speed, Accuracy, and Readiness for What’s Next

By leveraging TRES, Dune has not only accelerated its month-end closing process, but also laid the foundation for long-term treasury scalability. With a structured workflow in place and a reliable reconciliation engine behind it, the finance team can focus on high-level reporting, audits, and strategic decisions… not manual data collection.

As crypto accounting expectations evolve and regulatory standards tighten, Dune is already ahead of the curve – with a system that’s built for scale.

“TRES gives us the structure we need today – and the flexibility to grow tomorrow.” – Aleksander Langmyhr, Director of Finance at Dune.
