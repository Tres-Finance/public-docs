## What Is a Crypto Subledger?

A crypto subledger is specialized accounting software that connects blockchain activity with traditional finance systems like Xero, QuickBooks, NetSuite, SAP, or Oracle. Instead of manually exporting CSVs and reconciling transactions in spreadsheets, a subledger automatically:

- Collects data from wallets, exchanges, and custodians

- Categorizes transactions (swaps, staking, NFT sales, airdrops)

- Reconciles balances with on-chain reality

- Syncs results directly into your ERP

In short: a crypto subledger is the only way finance teams can keep crypto accounting accurate, compliant, and audit-ready in 2025.

## Why Crypto Accounting Is Hard Without a Subledger

Most finance teams discover the same issues when they try to handle crypto accounting manually:

- Thousands of transactions across multiple wallets and chains

- DeFi activity that standard accounting tools can’t interpret

- Staking and rewards that don’t reconcile with custodial statements

- Audit pressure to provide clean, verifiable trails

- ERP gaps, since traditional systems don’t understand on-chain data

Without a crypto subledger, accountants waste hours on manual reconciliation and risk major compliance issues. With one, finance teams can close the books faster, satisfy auditors, and eliminate errors.
