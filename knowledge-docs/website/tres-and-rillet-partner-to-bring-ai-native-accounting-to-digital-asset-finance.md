# TRES and Rillet Partner to Bring AI-Native Accounting to Digital Asset Finance

Managing digital assets and closing the books have always been two separate workstreams. On-chain activity reconciled in one place, core accounting happening somewhere else. For crypto-native companies and institutions with digital asset exposure, that disconnect has been the norm.

TRES has partnered with Rillet, the AI-native ERP, to change that. TRES is the first digital asset platform to integrate with Rillet – connecting on-chain financial data directly to the ERP layer, and enabling AI-powered accounting workflows that weren’t possible for digital asset companies before.

### How It Works

TRES handles the digital asset side of your financial operations – reconciliation, reporting, and audit-ready books across any blockchain. Rillet manages your core accounting workflows, with an AI-native architecture that automates General Ledger sync, reconciliations, and month-end close. Rillet’s continuous close model means your team starts day one of the month already working toward close – not scrambling at the end of it.

Together, your digital asset activity flows directly into your ERP. TRES pulls in your Rillet chart of accounts so your mapping rules point at real General Ledger codes, and every transaction you reconcile in TRES is composed into a proper Rillet journal entry – dated, coded, balanced – and posted straight into your ledger. Sandbox and production are respected end to end.

### Why We Partnered

The tools to run a modern finance operation exist. What’s been missing for digital asset companies is a connected stack where those tools actually work together. This partnership brings that together – an AI-native ERP and an AI-powered digital asset platform, natively integrated.

For crypto-native finance teams and institutions scaling their digital asset exposure, your financial operations now run on one connected system.

Tal Zackon, CEO of TRES:

“The future of financial operations is agentic, and this is how we’re building toward it. TRES powers the digital asset layer – clean data, automated reconciliation, audit-ready reporting – and through our partnership with Rillet, that intelligence now extends across your entire accounting stack.”

Nicolas Kopp, CEO of Rillet:

“The goal has always been to make Zero-Day Close a reality. Our partnership with TRES is a meaningful step toward that. Digital asset activity flows straight into Rillet’s intelligent ledger, AI handles the reconciliation, and teams get to spend their time on work that actually matters.”
