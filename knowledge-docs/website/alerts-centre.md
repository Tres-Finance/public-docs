## Enhance security with proactive transaction monitoring.

- Detect suspicious activities early by setting alerts for unusual transaction patterns or large transfers.

- Inactive wallets are at higher risk for unnoticed unauthorized transactions, making alerts essential for quickly detecting and responding to any unexpected activity.

## Maintain operational integrity by monitoring all relevant on-chain events.

- Configure alerts to flag transactions involving high-risk assets, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and enhancing scrutiny.

- Track non-supported token transactions to reduce operational challenges, as these tokens may not be fully integrated or recognized within your system.

## Mitigate risks by staying informed of slashing events.

- Stay on top of your Ethereum validators with 24/7 monitoring of slashing events in real-time.

- Bring peace of mind to delegators by implementing advanced controls.

## Proactively control your portfolio's risk exposure.

- Effectively manage risk by adjusting your portfolio when a specific asset exceeds or falls below a set percentage.

- Ensure your portfolio ratios remain aligned with your strategy by rebalancing when shifts occur due to market movements.
