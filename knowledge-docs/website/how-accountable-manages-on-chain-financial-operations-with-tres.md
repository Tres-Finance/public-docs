# How Accountable Manages On-Chain Financial Operations with TRES

### Overview

Accountable is a financial verification platform enabling institutions to prove assets and liabilities privately, without exposing sensitive details like API keys, wallet addresses, or trading strategies. Built for both traditional and crypto-native markets, their platform serves stablecoin issuers, asset managers, and brokers – with the majority of finance operations happening on-chain across 15 wallets and 6 networks.

### The Challenge

As Accountable built out its on-chain infrastructure, the finance team needed a reliable way to translate on-chain activity into fiat values and maintain an accurate inventory of historical balances across exchanges, wallets, and DeFi protocols.

“The main challenge is efficiently and reliably translating on-chain activity into fiat values,” said Jerome Tijssen, CFO at Accountable. “Besides that you need a trustworthy inventory system capable of extracting historical balances.”

### The Solution

Jerome found TRES through his network and the decision was straightforward. “I immediately felt they were – and still are – the benchmark in the industry,” he said.

TRES stood out for its coverage across a wide range of networks, including many less familiar ones – a factor that remains critical as Accountable’s operations grow. “We didn’t and still don’t want accounting tools and controls to limit our business operations,” said Jerome.

Accountable has used TRES since the company’s inception. The team relies on it for data entry into QuickBooks, day-to-day reconciliation across exchanges, wallets, and DeFi protocols, and month-end closing exports – with Ledger, Reports, Integrations, and Automations as their most-used features.

### The Impact

By replacing manual processes with TRES, Accountable has saved between 4 and 8 hours every week, freeing the finance team to focus on higher-value work.

“I can’t imagine working at a Web3 company without a subledger like TRES,” said Jerome. “Massive reduction in time spent on data entry and controls – especially when using automations.”

The reliability of TRES’s historical price feed has been equally important. “As most Web3 companies move their treasury on-chain, you need something that gives you a sense of control. TRES is doing exactly that.”

### Looking Ahead

As Accountable expands across more wallets and networks, reliable on-chain visibility remains essential to both finance operations and compliance.

“TRES stands out in finding innovative ways to make life much easier for CFOs and Finance Managers at a consistent, fast pace,” said Jerome Tijssen. “Combined with a wide range of motivated and responsive professionals.”
