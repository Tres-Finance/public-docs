## Real-time alerts to stay informed of critical events

TRES’ engineering team has built a powerful engine capable of informing clients based on any blockchain data point. Currently, TRES Alerts Centre allows users to create three types of alerts.

Transaction Alerts notifies users when transactions exceed or fall below predefined thresholds. This functionality allows teams to monitor large or unusual movements across specific wallets, ensuring better management of liquidity and financial risks.

Slashing Alerts track slashing penalties in Ethereum staking environments. By receiving real-time notifications, validators are better equipped to react swiftly and minimize potential losses.

Asset Exposure Alerts allow teams to set percentage or nominal thresholds for specific assets within their portfolio. This ensures that portfolios remain aligned with strategic goals, alerting managers when adjustments are needed.

## And the best part? This is only the beginning…

We’re continuously evolving our product suite to meet the growing needs of our clients. In the coming months, you can expect additional alert functionalities, including:

- First Interaction: Triggered when a user interacts with a wallet or asset for the first time.

- Rewards in a Position: Activated when rewards in a position exceed a specified amount.

- Asset Price Changes: Alerts when the price of a specific asset fluctuates beyond a certain percentage.

- Fiat / Crypto Ratio: Monitors and alerts when asset ratios (e.g., 90/10 between assets or fiat vs. crypto) deviate from the set target.

- Net Worth: Notifies when net worth goes above or below a specified amount.

- Capital Gains: Alerts when capital gains (realized and/or unrealized) on an asset or group of assets exceed or fall below a set threshold.
