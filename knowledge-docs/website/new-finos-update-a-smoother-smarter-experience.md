# New FinOS Update: A Smoother, Smarter Experience

Enterprise finance teams managing digital assets need tools that adapt to their workflow, not the other way around. That’s why we’ve released a set of new FinOS updates designed to make the platform faster, smarter, and easier to use.

## Stay Connected with Smart Email Notifications

Never miss an update again. FinOS now lets you configure email notifications that keep your team informed without overwhelming your inbox.

You can now get notified the moment your reports are generated and ready for review. Soon, we’re adding data collection alerts and reconciliation updates to keep your team in sync across all critical processes.

FinOS comes to you. No more checking back to see when things are ready.

## Organize your reports with the ‘Favorites’ Tab

Transform how you navigate your most critical reports. The new Favorites functionality lets you star your essential reports and access them instantly through a dedicated tab.

Create the report once and use it forever. Choose the type, select the filters and a name.

## Master Complex Transactions with Splitting and Grouping

Handle sophisticated payment scenarios with precision. Our new transaction splitting capability addresses a common challenge in enterprise finance – managing single payments that cover multiple obligations.

Real-world application:

- Multi-Invoice Payments: Split a single payment across three different supplier invoices

- Granular Tracking: Connect each split to its corresponding obligation

- Audit Trail Clarity: Maintain perfect records for complex payment structures

This feature transforms how you handle bulk payments, subscription renewals, and multi-vendor transactions – bringing clarity to complexity.
