# How to Switch to Accounting View

Switch between the standard ledger view and a simplified accounting view for easier transaction tracking and analysis.

## What you'll learn
This guide shows you how to toggle your ledger display to show transactions in an accounting format, which provides a cleaner, more focused view of your financial data.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to your Ledger
Go to the Ledger section in your Tres Finance dashboard.

![Navigate to Ledger](screenshots/step_01.png)

### Step 2: Find the view toggle switch
Look for the toggle switch located next to the "Transactions" tab at the top of the ledger page. This small switch controls which view format you see.

![Locate toggle switch](screenshots/step_02.png)

### Step 3: View your accounting format
Once you toggle the switch, your ledger will display in the simplified accounting view, making it easier to track your transactions with a cleaner layout.

![Accounting view active](screenshots/step_03.png)

---

## What's next?
Your view preference will be saved for future visits. You can toggle back to the standard view anytime using the same switch. The accounting view is particularly useful for users who prefer traditional accounting-style transaction displays.
