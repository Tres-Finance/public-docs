# How to Filter Transactions by Date

Filter your ledger transactions by specific date ranges to find transactions from particular time periods. This helps you analyze your financial activity within targeted timeframes.

## Steps

### Step 1: Access the Ledger with All Transactions

Navigate to your Ledger page to view all transactions across all time periods. The ledger will initially display every transaction in your account history.

![Step 1](screenshots/step_01.png)

### Step 2: View All Transaction History  

When viewing "All Time" transactions, your ledger displays the complete history of all your financial activity without any date restrictions. This gives you a comprehensive view of your account.

![Step 2](screenshots/step_02.png)

### Step 3: Apply a Custom Date Filter

To filter transactions for a specific time period, you can set custom date parameters. For example, to view transactions from June 2024, the system will apply your selected date range.

![Step 3](screenshots/step_03.png)

### Step 4: Review Filtered Results

Your ledger now displays only the transactions that occurred within your selected date range. In this example, you can see only the transactions from June 2024, making it easier to analyze that specific period.

![Step 4](screenshots/step_04.png)

## Tips

- Use date filtering to focus on specific months, quarters, or custom periods for better financial analysis
- The "All Time" view gives you the complete picture of your transaction history
- Custom date ranges help you prepare reports for specific time periods
