# How to View Cost Basis

View your assets' cost basis and financial performance using your selected accounting method in the Tres Finance dashboard.

## What you'll learn
This guide shows you how to access and view your cost basis information, including fair market value, total inventory, cumulative cost, realized gains, and unrealized gains.

## Prerequisites
- You must have cost basis currencies configured in your account
- Active Tres Finance account with transaction data

## Steps

### 1. Navigate to the Ledger page
Go to the Ledger section of your dashboard by visiting the `/ledger` page or clicking on "Ledger" in your navigation menu.

![Step 1](screenshots/step_01.png)

### 2. Click on the Cost Basis tab
Once on the Ledger page, click the "Cost Basis" tab to switch to the cost basis view.

![Step 2](screenshots/step_02.png)

**Note**: If you don't see the Cost Basis tab or it appears disabled, you may need to configure your cost basis currencies first.

### 3. View your cost basis information
The Cost Basis page will display your financial performance metrics, including:
- Fair market value of your assets
- Total inventory
- Cumulative cost
- Realized gains and losses
- Unrealized gains and losses

![Step 3](screenshots/step_03.png)

## Next steps
- Configure your cost basis accounting method if needed
- Export your cost basis data for tax reporting
- Review individual asset cost basis details

Need help configuring your cost basis method? Look for the configuration link in the Cost Basis tab tooltip.
