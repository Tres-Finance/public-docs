# How to View Your Transactions

View and browse your complete transaction history in the Tres Finance dashboard.

## Overview
Access your transaction ledger to review all your financial activity, including transaction dates, types, amounts, and classifications. This helps you track your financial history and understand your account activity.

## Steps

### 1. Navigate to the Ledger
Go to the Ledger section in your Tres Finance dashboard.

![Navigate to Ledger](screenshots/step_01.png)

### 2. Access the Transactions Tab
Click on the "Transactions" tab to view your transaction list. This tab is selected by default when you first visit the Ledger page.

![Click Transactions Tab](screenshots/step_02.png)

### 3. Review Your Transaction History
Your transaction list will display all your transactions with comprehensive details including:
- Transaction date and time
- Transaction type and description
- Amount (both inflows and outflows)
- Classification tags
- Additional details like fees and sync status

![View Transaction List](screenshots/step_03.png)

## What You Can See
- **Date & Time**: When each transaction occurred
- **Transaction Details**: Type and description of each transaction
- **Amounts**: Inflow and outflow amounts for each transaction
- **Classification**: Tags that help categorize your transactions
- **Status Information**: ERP sync status and other relevant details

This comprehensive view helps you track and understand all your financial activity within the Tres Finance platform.
