# How to Export Accounts Report

Export your wallets and accounts data to a comprehensive report for analysis or record-keeping.

## Description
This guide walks you through the process of generating and downloading a custom report of your accounts data from the Tres Finance dashboard. You can configure various settings to customize the report to your specific needs.

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the Accounts page
Go to your Accounts page in the Tres Finance dashboard.

![Navigate to Accounts](screenshots/step_01.png)

*Access your accounts overview at: `https://dashboard.prod.tres.finance/accounts`*

### Step 2: Click the Export Report button
Look for the "Export Report" button in the top-right area of the page header and click it.

![Click Export Report](screenshots/step_02.png)

### Step 3: Configure export settings and download
A configuration modal will open where you can:
- Set a custom report name
- Select specific filter options
- Choose export format and settings
- Configure any additional report parameters

Once you've configured your preferences, click the generate/download button to create your accounts report.

![Configure and Download](screenshots/step_03.png)

---

**Note**: The export process may take a few moments depending on the amount of data being processed. You'll receive your report file once generation is complete.
