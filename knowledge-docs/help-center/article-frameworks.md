# Frameworks Page

### Overview

The Frameworks page is your hub for specialized compliance and reporting tools. It provides structured workflows to help your organization meet complex regulatory obligations with ease.

Currently, the page features the 1099 Form framework for U.S. tax compliance, with additional frameworks for audit readiness and European regulations (MiCA, DORA) coming soon.

Key Capabilities:

Generate Tax Forms: Create IRS-compliant 1099-MISC and 1099-NEC forms for crypto transactions.

Automate Reporting: Use guided workflows to collect and validate data automatically.

Track Compliance: Monitor the completion status of every form with clear progress indicators.

### Available Frameworks

#### 1. 1099 Form (Active)

This framework simplifies U.S. tax compliance by guiding you through the generation of IRS 1099 forms for cryptocurrency transactions.

Key Features:

Automated Data Collection: Pulls relevant data directly from your TRES transaction ledger.

Multiple Form Support: Supports both 1099-MISC (Miscellaneous Income) and 1099-NEC (Non-Employee Compensation).

Historical Tracking: Organized by tax year (e.g., 2024, 2025) for easy reference.

Export Ready: Download completed forms for filing with the IRS.

Learn more about 1099 form here.

#### Upcoming Frameworks:
