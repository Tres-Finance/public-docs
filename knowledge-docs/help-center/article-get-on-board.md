# Get on Board

At TRES, we know the role of finance leaders is more complex than ever. You’re reconciling wallets, monitoring trading activity, preparing for audits, and reporting across multiple jurisdictions. That’s why TRES onboarding is simple, fast, and secure - giving you immediate access to audit-ready financial data.

### Step 1: Accept your invitation

Check your inbox for an email from TRES Finance. It will look like this:

Click Accept Invitation. This links your profile to your organization’s secure TRES account.

### Step 2: Create your password

After accepting the invitation, you’ll be directed to the TRES sign-up page. Choose a strong password to protect your account, then hit Continue.

Behind the scenes, your access is protected by enterprise-grade security controls that keep sensitive financial data safe.

### Step 3: Log in and explore

Once your password is set, you’ll land on the TRES login page. This is how you access your control center. From here you’ll monitor wallets, reconcile transactions, and prepare audit-ready reports.

That's it. You're in.

### What you’ve just unlocked

By activating your account, you now have access to:

Continuous reconciliation across 250+ networks

Audit-ready data pipelines aligned with SOC standards

ERP integrations that turn digital asset activity into financial statements

Staking, DeFi, and derivatives coverage in one secure system

This is just the starting point. Welcome to the future of digital asset finance and compliance.
