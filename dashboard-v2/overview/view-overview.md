# How to View Your Portfolio Overview

Get a complete view of your investment portfolio, including your total net worth, asset allocation, and recent account activity.

## Description
The Portfolio Overview page is your financial dashboard home, providing a comprehensive snapshot of your investment performance and account activity in one convenient location.

## Steps

### Step 1: Access your Portfolio Overview
Navigate to your Portfolio Overview page, which serves as the main dashboard for your Tres Finance account.

![Portfolio Overview Navigation](screenshots/step_01.png)

### Step 2: Review your financial summary
Once on the overview page, you'll see three key sections:

- **Net Worth**: Your total portfolio value displayed prominently at the top
- **Asset Allocation**: Visual breakdown of how your investments are distributed across different asset types
- **Recent Activity**: A summary of your latest account transactions and changes

![Portfolio Overview Dashboard](screenshots/step_02.png)

## What's Next?
From the Portfolio Overview, you can dive deeper into specific areas of your portfolio or explore other sections of your dashboard to manage your investments.
