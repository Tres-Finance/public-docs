# How to Add a Manual Wallet

**Category**: Account Management  
**Description**: Learn how to add a manual blockchain wallet to your Tres Finance organization for tracking and management purposes.

---

## Overview

Manual wallets allow you to track blockchain addresses that you own but may not have direct integration with. This is useful for monitoring wallets from hardware devices, other platforms, or addresses you want to track for reporting purposes.

## Steps

### 1. Navigate to the Accounts page

Go to the Accounts section of your Tres Finance dashboard.

![Step 1](screenshots/step_01.png)

### 2. Open the Actions menu

Click the "Actions" dropdown button to see available account actions.

![Step 2](screenshots/step_02.png)

### 3. Select "Add wallet"

From the dropdown menu, choose "Add wallet" to start the wallet creation process.

![Step 3](screenshots/step_03.png)

### 4. Choose "Manual wallet" type

Select "Manual wallet" as your wallet type from the available options.

![Step 4](screenshots/step_04.png)

### 5. Enter wallet details

Fill in the required and optional information:

**Wallet name** (required): Enter a descriptive name for your wallet

![Step 5](screenshots/step_05.png)

**Description** (optional): Add any additional details about this wallet

![Step 6](screenshots/step_06.png)

**Wallet address** (optional): Enter the blockchain address you want to track

![Step 7](screenshots/step_07.png)

### 6. Complete the setup

Click "Add Wallet" to create your manual wallet and add it to your organization.

![Step 8](screenshots/step_08.png)

---

**Note**: Manual wallets are read-only and used for tracking purposes. You won't be able to perform transactions directly through Tres Finance with these wallets.
