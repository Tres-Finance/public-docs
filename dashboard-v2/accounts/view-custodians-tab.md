# How to View Custodian Integrations

Monitor the sync status and configuration of your custodian integrations to ensure your account data stays up-to-date.

## What you'll see
View details about your connected custodians including sync status, last update times, linked wallets, and data types being synchronized.

## Steps

### 1. Go to your Accounts page
Navigate to the main Accounts section of your dashboard.

![Navigate to Accounts](screenshots/step_01.png)

### 2. Select the Custodians tab
Click on the "Custodians" tab to view your integration settings.

![Click Custodians tab](screenshots/step_02.png)

### 3. Review your custodian integrations
You'll see a comprehensive view of all your custodian connections, including:
- Integration status and last sync time
- Connected entities and wallet addresses
- Data types being synchronized
- Configuration details

![View custodian details](screenshots/step_03.png)

This page helps you monitor whether your custodian data is syncing properly and troubleshoot any connection issues.
