# How to View and Manage Your Contacts

View and organize all your saved blockchain addresses in one convenient location.

## Overview
The Contacts feature in your Tres Finance dashboard allows you to store and manage frequently used blockchain addresses. This makes it easy to send transactions to trusted recipients without having to remember or re-enter long wallet addresses.

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the Accounts section

Go to the Accounts page in your dashboard.

![Navigate to Accounts](screenshots/step_01.png)

### Step 2: Switch to the Contacts tab

Click on the "Contacts" tab to access your saved addresses.

![Click Contacts tab](screenshots/step_02.png)

### Step 3: View your contact list

You'll see all your saved contacts displayed in an organized list. The contacts are grouped alphabetically and include search functionality to help you quickly find specific addresses.

![View contacts list](screenshots/step_03.png)

## What You Can Do

Once you're viewing your contacts, you can:
- Search for specific contacts using the search bar
- Browse contacts organized alphabetically
- View contact details and associated blockchain addresses
- Use pagination to navigate through large contact lists

## Next Steps

From the Contacts page, you can also add new contacts, edit existing ones, or use saved addresses when sending transactions. This helps streamline your workflow and reduces the risk of errors when entering recipient addresses.
