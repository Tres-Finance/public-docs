# How to View Transaction Details

Learn how to expand transactions on the Ledger page to see detailed subtransaction information.

## Description
When viewing your transaction history in the Ledger, you can expand any transaction to see its individual subtransactions and detailed breakdown. This feature helps you understand the complete flow of funds for complex transactions.

## Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to the Ledger page
Go to the Ledger section of your Tres Finance dashboard to view your transaction history.

![Step 1](screenshots/step_01.png)

### Step 2: Click on a transaction row to expand it
Click anywhere on the transaction row you want to examine in detail. You'll see a caret icon rotate to indicate the transaction is expanding.

![Step 2](screenshots/step_02.png)

### Step 3: Review the expanded transaction details
The transaction will expand to show a detailed subtransaction table with information about amounts, source and destination accounts, gains, and other relevant details.

![Step 3](screenshots/step_03.png)

### Step 4: Click the transaction row again to collapse the details
To hide the detailed view, simply click on the same transaction row again. The details will collapse back to the summary view.

![Step 4](screenshots/step_04.png)

## Tips
- You can have multiple transactions expanded at the same time
- The caret icon shows the expansion state - pointing right when collapsed, pointing down when expanded
- All transaction data in the expanded view can help you track the complete flow of your funds
