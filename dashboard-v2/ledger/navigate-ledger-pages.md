# How to Navigate Between Ledger Pages

Navigate through multiple pages of transactions using the pagination controls in your Tres Finance ledger.

## Overview
When you have more than 10 transactions, the ledger will automatically display pagination controls at the bottom of the transaction list, allowing you to browse through all your transaction data efficiently.

## Steps

### Step 1: Access Your Ledger
Navigate to the Ledger page in your Tres Finance dashboard to view your transaction history.

![Step 1](screenshots/step_01.png)

### Step 2: Locate the Pagination Controls
Scroll to the bottom of the transaction list to find the pagination controls. These controls appear automatically when you have more than 10 transactions.

![Step 2](screenshots/step_02.png)

The pagination controls include:
- Previous/Next buttons for moving between pages
- Page numbers for jumping to specific pages
- Page size selector to change how many transactions display per page

### Step 3: Navigate to the Next Page
Click the "Next" button or a specific page number to view additional transactions. Notice that the URL updates to reflect the current page (e.g., `?page=2`).

![Step 3](screenshots/step_03.png)

### Step 4: Return to Previous Pages
Use the "Previous" button to navigate back to earlier pages of transactions. The button will be disabled when you're on the first page.

![Step 4](screenshots/step_04.png)

## Tips
- The pagination controls only appear when you have more than 10 total transactions
- You can bookmark specific page URLs to return directly to that view
- Use the page size selector to display more transactions per page if preferred
